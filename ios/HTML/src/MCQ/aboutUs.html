About Cambridge English Qualifications – BEC Business

A suite of three English language qualification certifications for international business - these qualifications are in-depth exams, each focussing on a level of the Common European Framework of Reference (CEFR), enabling learners to develop and build speaking, writing, reading and listening skills.

  • BEC B1 Business Preliminary
  • BEC B2 Business Vantage
  • BEC C1 Business Higher

Over 1 million people have used these certificates to show that they have the work-focussed English language skills for career success. Know more
