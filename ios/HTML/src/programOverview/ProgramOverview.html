The EnglishEdge Apps aims at providing working professionals, graduates and all those who believe in practical learning to become confident, effective and reflective as they face various challenges.

The program follows EnglishEdge's unique New Age CAPE methodology - Concept, Activity, Practice & Evaluation.

This methodology gives learner the opportunity to learn the concept or practice the concept at various times.

The learner will be exposed to various learning environments which may include:

The Self-learning CONCEPT These videos will help the learner to understand the concepts through scenarios. The audio/visual aids the various learning styles.

The Quiz Questions Simple to complex MCQs stimulate the learner's knowledge and curiosity to know more while the learning happens subtly.

Vocabulary Enhancement Vocabulary will help the learners to know the meaning and pronunciation of the skill related words. This feature provides learners with a unique opportunity to record their voices and compare with pre-recorded expert voice. This will enhance their pronunciation and built on their confidence.

The learning gained thus will directly impact the learner's daily life by improving the quality of Communication.

Each of these components emphasize on the overall personality development of the learner.
